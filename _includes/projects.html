Hand Gesture Replication Robotic Arm

Arduino controlled robotic arm to replicate the human hand. Motion sensors like accelerometers, gyro sensors and flex sensors were used to take signals from the human hand. XBee modules were used for wireless communication. Uses Prediction and Correction algorithm for precise replication. GUI using visual studio was built as an alternative to send signals to the robotic arm.
